hi, my name is ada

i'm an artist, technician, and dj. my job is designing and operating the lighting at clubs and concerts. i also create live glitch visuals using old analogue video hardware, and i'm a programmer with a degree in computer science and a lot of hobbyist electronics experience. i have been performing live music since i was a child and have been helping run club nights and club night-adjacent events since 2021. i am a former station manager of subcity radio and also the one who made their website.

this site contains information about me, my work, and things that i like. it's been written by hand in plain html, css, and the tiniest bit of javascript.

i would love to chat to you! you can find my contact details at the bottom of this page.
