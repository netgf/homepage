my homepage has a variety of 88x31 buttons, some of which link to cool sites you should visit. the bitrot webring is a directory of cool people i know who have sites. i won't necessarily exclude or include any of the sites that appear in either of those places here, but it's fun to explore them independently of this list. no particular order here, just stuff i feel like linking to

chaos computer club media archive

media.ccc.de

i really like what the chaos computer club, a large german (+swiss+austrian+etc) hacker organisation, are up to. a lot of people associated with the ccc are doing a lot of cool things. this site features talks from their events in both german and english
